5. **Critical schema mismatch found** -- `RoleName` has custom `Serialize`/`Deserialize` impls that serialize to a **string** (`"Engineer"`), but the derived `JsonSchema` describes an **object** with `original` and `lowercase` fields. Any `jsonschema` validation of role JSON against the derived `Role` schema will falsely fail on the `name` field.

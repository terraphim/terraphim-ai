Strategic Opportunities

Terraphim Graph Ranking is a key differentiator in custom-built stage. Focus on evolving this to product stage for competitive advantage.

Market Position

Privacy-first positioning exploits weakness in cloud-based AI assistants. Local processing addresses growing privacy concerns.

Evolution Pressure

Knowledge graph integration and role-based personas are evolving toward standardization. Consider productization strategy.

Dependencies

Heavy reliance on existing tools (Obsidian, Logseq) creates both opportunity and risk. Integration quality is critical.

Competitive Moats

Local execution, privacy preservation, and personalized knowledge graphs create defensible positioning.

Investment Focus

Prioritize Terraphim Graph evolution and seamless PKM integrations to maintain differentiation.

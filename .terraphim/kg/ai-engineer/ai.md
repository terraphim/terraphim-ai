# AI Engineer Knowledge Graph

## Large Language Models

Large Language Models are deep learning models trained on vast text corpora for natural language understanding and generation.

[synonyms::] llm, transformer, gpt, claude, gemini, llama

## Prompt Engineering

Prompt engineering designs effective inputs to guide LLM behaviour and output quality.

[synonyms::] prompt, system-prompt, few-shot, chain-of-thought, prompt-template

## Retrieval-Augmented Generation

RAG combines information retrieval with text generation to ground LLM outputs in external knowledge.

[synonyms::] rag, retrieval, embedding, vector-store, knowledge-graph, grounding

## Fine-Tuning

Fine-tuning adapts pre-trained models to specific tasks or domains with additional training data.

[synonyms::] fine-tune, lora, qlora, adapter, peft, instruction-tuning

## Embeddings

Embeddings map discrete tokens or entities into dense vector spaces for similarity search.

[synonyms::] embedding, vector, cosine-similarity, semantic-search, dense-vector

## Agents

AI agents are autonomous systems that use tools, planning, and memory to accomplish goals.

[synonyms::] agent, tool-use, function-calling, planning, reasoning, orchestration

## Evaluation

Evaluation measures model performance through benchmarks, human judgement, and automated metrics.

[synonyms::] eval, benchmark, perplexity, bleu, rouge, human-eval

## Inference Optimisation

Inference optimisation reduces latency and cost for model serving.

[synonyms::] inference, quantisation, pruning, distillation, vllm, tensorrt

## Multi-Modal Models

Multi-modal models process and generate across text, image, audio, and video modalities.

[synonyms::] multimodal, vision, speech, clip, diffusion, stable-diffusion

## Responsible AI

Responsible AI addresses bias, fairness, transparency, and safety in AI systems.

[synonyms::] bias, fairness, explainability, safety, alignment, red-teaming

## Knowledge Graphs

Knowledge graphs represent structured knowledge as entities and relationships for reasoning.

[synonyms::] knowledge-graph, ontology, rdf, entity, relation, graph-rag

## MLOps

MLOps operationalises machine learning models from training to production monitoring.

[synonyms::] mlops, model-serving, drift, monitoring, pipeline, feature-store
